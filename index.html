OUR STORY

The Pirates Legacy

Founded in 1987 by a group of rugby enthusiasts in Kampala, Pirates Uganda Rugby Club has grown to become one of the most successful and respected rugby clubs in East Africa.

Our name "Pirates" reflects our fearless approach to the game - we take what we want on the pitch through skill, determination, and teamwork. But off the pitch, we're a family committed to developing young talent and promoting rugby throughout Uganda.
